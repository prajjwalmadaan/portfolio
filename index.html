"The cockpit demands precision, calm, and an unwavering commitment to safety — values I have been building toward my entire life."

I am Prajjwal Madaan, an 18-year-old aspiring commercial airline pilot from Sonipat, Haryana. From an early age, aviation captured my imagination — and over time, that fascination has grown into a deliberate, methodical pursuit of a professional flying career.

I have successfully cleared two DGCA CPL-level examinations — Air Regulations and Aviation Meteorology — demonstrating both a thorough understanding of aviation fundamentals and the self-discipline to study at a level well beyond my current training stage.

My academic results in Physics and Mathematics, competitive achievements in table tennis at state level, and school leadership experience as Vice House Captain have each shaped the attributes I consider most critical in a future commercial pilot: analytical thinking, calm under pressure, and the ability to lead and communicate effectively.

I am driven by purpose, prepared through discipline, and committed to the professional standards that define excellence in commercial aviation.
